# ERCF mount top rear

mount for the ercf on top of the v2.4, below the filament spools

## requirements

- 2x 2020 profiles in 420mm length for 300mm v2.4
- 4x M5/M6 20mm SHCS depending on your 420mm 2020 profiles
- 6x M5 20mm SHCS
- 6x M5 2020 T-Nuts
